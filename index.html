Our Concept

We believe food should be as beautiful to look at as it is delicious to eat. Our grazing tables are immersive, edible experiences — thoughtfully curated spreads where every element, from the flavors to the florals, tells a story. Each dish is presented in mini individual servings, inviting guests to graze freely, explore flavors at their own pace, and discover something new with every bite. It’s food designed for connection — casual, abundant, and effortlessly elegant.

We go beyond food styling to create a visual and sensory experience — blending decor, color, and floral design to complement the mood of your celebration. Whether playful or sophisticated, every table is a work of art that invites guests to linger, engage, and savor. Because for us, it’s never just a table — it’s a conversation piece, a celebration of culture and creativity, and a memory waiting to be made. 🌸

About Me

For over two decades, I’ve built my career as a software engineer — a field that’s taught me the value of precision, creativity, and thoughtful design. Years of problem-solving and innovation have shaped how I see the world: through detail, balance, and intention. Alongside that professional journey, my lifelong passions for food and travel have always been quietly shaping another part of me — the part that finds joy in flavor, beauty, and shared experiences. I’ve always believed that food is more than just taste. It first captures your eye, then draws you in with its aroma, and finally delights you with its flavor. A beautifully styled table doesn’t just serve food — it invites, connects, and elevates.

From experimenting with recipes to hosting friends and family, I’ve found immense joy in creating moments that linger long after the last bite. And now, I’m bringing together my professional discipline and creative curiosity to launch something deeply personal — The Urban India Grazing Co. This venture is where my love for aesthetics, travel, and flavor meet my technical precision and strong work ethic. Every grazing table is designed with intention — blending Indian warmth with modern global flair to turn gatherings into experiences that are as visually stunning as they are delicious.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a grazing table?
A grazing table is an abundant, beautifully styled food display featuring a curated mix of appetizers, mains, and desserts — designed for guests to pick, taste, and enjoy at their own pace. It's part art, part dining experience — perfect for making gatherings feel relaxed, elegant, and social.
What kind of food do you offer?
We create Indian-inspired grazing tables with a global twist. Our menu includes a variety of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options — from small bites and mains to desserts — all beautifully presented to please the eye and the palate.
Do you customize the menu?
Absolutely! Every grazing table is customized to match your event theme, dietary preferences, and style — whether it's elegant, festive, minimal, or traditional.
Do you cater for vegetarian or vegan guests?
Yes! We can create fully vegetarian, vegan, or mixed menus, and clearly label each section so your guests can graze confidently.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends or special occasions, to ensure availability and time for menu customization.
Do you provide setup and styling?
Yes! Each grazing table is fully styled and set up by our team for an additional cost. We handle the setup, styling, and food placement — you just show up and enjoy!

We can also rent out food warmers and related display equipment if needed. Reach out to discuss your setup and styling requirements.
What's included in the setup?
We provide all serving platters, boards, props, and decor elements. We can also tailor the design with your event theme or color palette for a cohesive look.
Do you offer delivery or pickup options?
Yes! We provide delivery within 40 miles for an additional cost. You are also welcome to pick up your order directly if that's more convenient.
Where are you based, and how far do you travel?
We are based in the Lakeview Area in Chicago. Travel within 40 miles is available with an additional fee for delivery or setup.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Yes — we take allergies and dietary needs seriously. Please let us know in advance about any restrictions, and we'll customize your menu accordingly.
Do you cater for corporate or large events?
We are starting slow and steady. At the moment, we have the capacity to curate grazing tables for up to 40 guests. As we grow, we look forward to expanding our capacity and menu offerings.
Do you require a deposit to book?
Yes, a 50% deposit secures your date. The remaining balance is due one week before the event.
What is your cancellation policy?
We understand that plans can change.
  • Within 48 hours of placing your order: 100% of your total payment is refundable.
  • Up to 1 week before your event: 50% of your total payment is refundable.
  • Less than 7 days before your event: No refunds are issued, as we've already invested our time, planning, and ingredients into curating your experience.

We appreciate your understanding — every grazing table is designed with love, time, and care.
Can I collaborate or feature The Urban India Grazing Co. at my event?
We love creative collaborations — from styled shoots to partnerships with local brands. Get in touch and let's create something beautiful together!
How do I get started?
Simply fill out our inquiry form or message us with your event details — date, location, number of guests, and theme — and we'll design a grazing experience just for you.
